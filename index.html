Watt Does This Tracker Do?

The Energy Counter is a website used by over 90 countries worldwide. It's popularity has grown in recent years due to its consumer-friendly interface and ease of accessibility. The technology is simple yet it's utility has helped households, hospitals and large businesses reduce their consumption and save costs. By using The Energy Counter, not only are you increasing your savings, you're also contributing to a cleaner, more sustainable environment.
